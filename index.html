Who We Are

Get to know our roots

Café Aroma began as a small neighborhood nook where passion for bold brews met a love for good conversation. We created a space for connection, comfort, and community.

Now a local favorite, our café welcomes everyone — students, artists, professionals — with warm lighting, smooth playlists, and of course, fantastic coffee.
